Personal Information Documentation

One of the things I enjoy doing is creating innovative solutions using data to solve issues that plague society. I love thinking about issues like global warming, global hunger, homelessness, and poverty, and different scenario that these issues could be solved. Therefore, in order to one day be part of the team that helps put an end to this issue, I decided to study an intriguing course at Nova Scotia Community College.

My name is Esther Obieme. I am an African Nova Scotia Student studying IT Database Administration at Nova Scotia Community College. I have recently completed my first year for this course, and patiently waiting for my second and last year studying Database Administration.

Data is an ideology that exists everywhere. It is used to make predictions about the possible future, the impact the future would have on humanity and society, and how to counter them effectively.

Data has changed and is still changing the world at a rapid paste. My goal is to learn how to collect, store, and manipulate data in order to be prepared to solve uprising issues.
